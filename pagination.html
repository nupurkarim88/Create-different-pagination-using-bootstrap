Let us learn about Pagination Today!

In web development, "pagination" is a technique where large amounts of content are divided into smaller, manageable pages, allowing users to navigate through information by clicking on numbered pages or "next" and "previous" buttons, essentially preventing users from being overwhelmed by a massive amount of data on a single page.
